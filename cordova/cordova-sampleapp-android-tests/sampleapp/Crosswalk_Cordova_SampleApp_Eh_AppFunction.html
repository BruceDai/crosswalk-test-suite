Preconditions:

  1. A good network which is able to access to google service
  2. Two google accounts and make sure they are contacts for each other
  3. Two android devices which are able to access to google service and signed in with the above google accounts
  4. Install 'Eh' sample app in the two android devices(Refer to 'README' file)
  5. One host connected to the network to run GCM server
  6. Install python in the host
  7. Put GCM server(https://github.com/MobileChromeApps/workshop-cca-eh/tree/master/gcmServer) in the host
  8. Set 'username' with '585199982707' and set 'password' with 'AIzaSyCXxKFxOTwdkp5I6V1858TF_5d0m4wk9Ps' in gcmServer/gcm_auth_info.json
  9. Start the GCM server using 'python gcmServer/python/server.py'

Test steps:

  1. Launch 'Eh' sample app in the first android device
  2. An authorization prompt might appear, then click "OK" button to allow app and google to use your google account
  3. Check if the app title shows "It's {Username of google account}, eh"(e.g. "It's crosswalktest1, eh")
  4. Launch 'Eh' sample app in the second android device
  5. An authorization prompt might appear, then click "OK" button to allow app and google to use your google account
  6. Check if the app title shows "It's {Username of google account}, eh"(e.g. "It's crosswalktest2, eh")
  7. Check the two devices if the app lists the username of its contact
  8. Click the username of its contact in the first android device
  9. Check the message(e.g. "Received eh: 2 minutes ago") under it's contact in the second android device

Expected Output:

  1. The app title shows "It's {Username of google account}, eh"(e.g. "It's crosswalktest1, eh")
  2. The app title shows "It's {Username of google account}, eh"(e.g. "It's crosswalktest2, eh")
  3. The first android device lists the the username of its contact(e.g. "crosswalktest2")
  4. The second android device lists the username of its contact(e.g. "crosswalktest1")
  5. The second android device receives one message(e.g. "Received eh: 2 minutes ago") from the first android device
