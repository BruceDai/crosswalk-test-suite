pre_condition: 1.Make sure Crosswalk binary is installed; 2.Make sure web_feature_scheme_tests.apk is installed; 3.Make sure at least one Map Application is installed; 4.Make sure there at least 1 image in the device sdcard. 5.Make sure network can be used.

purpose: Validate the scheme 'app://', 'mailto:', 'market:', 'sms:', 'tel:', 'geo:' and 'content://' are implemented in Crosswalk

Test steps:

  1. Click app icon to launch app
  2. Click the link of "geo-1" to launch map app
  3. Click the link of "mailto-1" to launch email app
  4. Click the link of "Open Market" to launch Market app
  5. Click the link of "Send Sms" to launch message app
  6. Click the link of "Make call" to launch Tel app
  7. Install webapp by typing "adb install web_scheme_content_tests.apk"
  8. Click app icon to launch app
  9. Run command in terminal: "adb shell content query --uri content://media/external/images/media"
  10. Check the terminal and get a value of "_id".
  11. Set the "_id" value to "Image Id" textarea.
  12. Click the link of "Open Image".

Expected Output:

  1. The app launched successfully
  2. The map app could pop up
  3. The email app could pop up
  4. The Market app could pop up after click the link
  5. The Sms app could pop up after click the link
  6. The Tel app could pop up after click the link
  7. Webapp is installed successfully, no error, no warning message
  8. The app launched successfully
  9. There display the images' info about the device sdcard in terminal
  10. At least 1 "_id" exists.
  11. The "_id" value was set to "Image Id" textarea.
  12. There display the image.
